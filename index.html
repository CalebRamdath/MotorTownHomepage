Open World

Free roam open world and pick up hitchhikers. Don't forget to refuel once in a while and check coolant temperature. For every driving, you get experience point, which let you buy or drive more vehicles. Police vehicles are watching for any traffic violations. You can get out of vehicles and interact with residents or have a cup of coffee.

Driving Jobs

You can drive taxi, bus and trucks. And more special jobs will come in future. Comfort Driving mode is handy for some passengers who favor gentle driving and some cargo that can easily tip over.

Realistic Tire Physics

With sophisticated vehicle and tire physics, you can enjoy realistic and challenging driving. Feel the tire grip and burn some tires by drifting! If you are keyboard player, don't forget to use 'Burnout' when you want to let the rear go side way. Driving Assist is available for keyboard players and controllers are fully supported.

Singleplayer and Multiplayer

Enjoy solitude in singleplayer or have fun with friends in multiplayer mode.

Race and Time Attack

You can compete with AI or Other players at the Onroad and offroad tracks. Autocross is good way to improve your driving skills.

Leaderboard

Online leader board shows you how fast you are.
